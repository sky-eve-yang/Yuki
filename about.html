There are 3 key words to describe myself: empathic, initiative and self-challenging.
I'm accustomed to stepping out of my comfort zone and attempting new things in both academics and life. I'm also an excellent listener, a "tree hole" for those around me, and a fervent advocate of worthy causes both domestically and abroad.
Inspired by martial arts philosophy, I am used to speaking out for the underprivileged and neglected beings (birds in nature, dwindling wild leopards, struggling depressed patients, groups hit hard by the pandemic, Africans with underserved health care and electricity, and schoolgirls suffering from period poverty and period stigma).
