Practice and Perception

I'm severely addicted to "diving" into the realm of calligraphy and seal-cutting because it allows me to constantly experience the fluidity of each stroke, the vividness of each word, and the joy of sprinkling ink. I was five years old when I first became addicted.

I was inspired to refrain from doing the same in my own life by the necessity of never overfilling a stroke in calligraphy and of leaving some white space in some of the strokes. I've learned to cherish the times when my mind is uncluttered. In addition to appreciating life and nature while learning calligraphy, I've also come to value my alone time.
